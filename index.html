Tommy Vercetti

The man who rule over Miami Vice City

Here is the life of Tommy Vercetti

  • 1951: Tommy Vercetti is born in Liberty City.
  • Late 1960s/Early 1970s: Tommy begins working for the Forelli crime family in Liberty City.
  • 1971: Tommy is sent by Sonny Forelli to kill a rival mobster in the Harwood District. The setup turns into an ambush, and Tommy kills eleven men, earning the nickname "The Harwood Butcher." He is arrested and sentenced to 15 years in prison.
  • 1986: Tommy is released from prison. Sonny Forelli sends him to Vice City to oversee a drug deal. The deal goes bad, with an ambush resulting in the loss of the drugs and money, and the deaths of Tommy's associates.
  • 1986: Determined to recover the lost money and drugs, Tommy starts working for various criminal elements in Vice City, including Ricardo Diaz. He gradually builds his own criminal empire.
  • 1986: Tommy discovers that Ricardo Diaz was behind the ambush. Tommy and his associate Lance Vance kill Diaz, taking over his mansion and operations.
  • 1986: Tommy expands his criminal empire, taking control of various businesses in Vice City, including a nightclub, a strip club, and a film studio.
  • 1986: Lance Vance becomes increasingly paranoid and feels underappreciated. He betrays Tommy by colluding with Sonny Forelli.
  • 1986: Sonny Forelli arrives in Vice City to collect his money, leading to a climactic showdown at Tommy's mansion. Tommy kills Sonny, Lance, and the attacking Forelli hitmen, solidifying his control over Vice City.
  • Post-1986: Tommy establishes himself as the undisputed crime lord of Vice City, running a vast criminal empire with various illicit businesses.
  • Unknown Future: The exact details of Tommy's later life remain unknown, but he is presumed to continue his reign over Vice City's criminal underworld.

This timeline highlights the key events in Tommy Vercetti's life as depicted in the game "Grand Theft Auto: Vice City."
